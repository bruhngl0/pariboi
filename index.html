Case Study • 2026

02

Organizational Background

ProPlay Sports Pvt. Ltd. is a sport oriented business with its operations in the field of sport retailing and services in India. The company was founded in 2019 with the focus on online selling sports equipment, sponsorship programs of athletes, and marketing sports programs at the community level in the urban and semi-urban areas of India.

The company operates mostly based on an e-commerce model, which is backed by centralized procurement and a chain of regional warehouses in South and West India. ProPlay markets itself as a commercial sports venture and at the same time endorses ethical sporting behaviors, inclusivity and long term sporting talent development.

03

Business Environment

The sports business ecosystem in India has also been developing at a very fast pace, owing to the growing health consciousness, more people have moved towards digitalization and sports have become more commercialized. The growth of the e-commerce websites has increased the competition level that has made sports companies concentrate on pricing effectiveness, speedy delivery, and decision-making based on technological factors.

On the one hand, organizations have more opportunities to develop, but on the other hand, there are additional challenges of control in relation to the payment of taxes, expenditures on sponsorship and corporate social responsibility towards athletes and communities.

04

AI Integration at ProPlay

At ProPlay, AI-powered applications have been implemented to predict demand, control stock and customer behavior. These technological faculties have played a major role in the managerial decision making processes in the fields of finance, operations and internet trading.

Impact on Decision-Making

The integration of AI systems has fundamentally changed how ProPlay approaches business decisions, bringing both opportunities for optimization and new challenges in terms of governance and ethical oversight.

05

Current Managerial Situation

The management at ProPlay will be faced with numerous operational and strategic pressures by the end of FY 2025-26. The rapid development of online sales has led to the increase in volumes of procurement and the usage of warehouses, which causes the increase in storage costs and the rise in inventory risks.

The finance department has expressed issues of variable profit margins and income taxation of athlete sponsorship costs. Meanwhile, the operations team highlights the necessity of bulk purchases to cope with the volatility of the demand and service levels.

06

Leadership Challenges

The top management is equally under pressure to maintain the level of ethical sporting culture in ProPlay as more commercialization and competition mounts pressure based on the latter.

Financial Pressures

Variable profit margins and complex tax implications of sponsorship expenditures

Operational Demands

Balancing bulk procurement benefits against rising storage costs and inventory risks

Cultural Commitment

Maintaining ethical sporting values amid increasing commercial pressures

07

Interdisciplinary Decision Situation

The managerial challenge is complicated by the combination of various academic and functional approaches. Multiple disciplines intersect to create a complex decision-making environment that requires careful navigation and integration of different perspectives.

08

AI-Driven Decision Making

The AI systems offer predictive analytics in the sales trends, inventory optimization and procurement planning. Nevertheless, the growing use of the results of algorithmic decisions poses issues that are associated with transparency, managerial responsibility, and moral judgment.

The Challenge

How much should management rely on AI-generated insights versus human judgment when making strategic decisions?

09

E-Commerce Dynamics

Digital promotions and flash sales online attract a quick growth in revenue but pose challenges in long-term inventory planning, filling of orders, and the use of warehouses.

The rapid growth strategy through online channels creates operational complexities that must be managed carefully to maintain service quality while controlling costs.

10

Income Tax Implications

The tax implications of sponsorship expenditures made on athletes, depreciation of sports equipment and reporting of income present compliance issues and affect financial planning choices.

Relevant policy considerations include income tax provisions that apply in sponsorship costs and legal considerations that apply in online operations of the business.

11

Procurement, Storage & Warehouse Management

Bulk buying plans lower the per-unit costs of procuring products to the disadvantage of higher inventory holding costs and the danger of stagnant or slow moving products.

  • Increasing warehouse storage expenses in quarter to quarter
  • Growing ratio of overall sales made on digital promotions
  • Increasing sponsorship spending that affect the taxable income levels
  • Predictions of AI-based predictions with high seasonal demand volatility
12

Sports Business Finance

Investments in athlete sponsorships, the infrastructure and the digital growth have trade-offs that directly impact profitability and the financial sustainability in the long-term.

Resource Allocation Dilemma

Management must decide how to distribute limited financial resources across competing priorities while maintaining both commercial viability and social commitments.

13

Contemporary Issues in Sporting Culture

Commercial success and fair play preservation versus the promotion of athlete welfare and growth of grassroots sports give the managerial decision-making a social and ethical angle.

These related issues, along with others, pose conflicting priorities and restraints to the management.

14

Indian Knowledge Systems Perspective

The leadership philosophy of ProPlay is based on the Indian idea of Dharma or the ethical accountability and moderation of business practices. The principle of Trusteeship also finds its way into the organization as the organization considers profits just as a way of supporting athletes, communities and the greater sporting ecosystem and not necessarily as a way of accumulation.

These values are the ones that affect the debate on internal management about whether aggressive growth policies are in line with the traditional Indian principles of fairness, discipline, and social contribution by sports.

15

Sustainability Considerations

The managerial dilemma is developed under the influence of sustainability considerations in the following ways:

  • Environmental sustainability: More packaging and the overall emissions related to logistics that come as a result of e-commerce activities
  • Social sustainability: Ongoing funding of youth sports and inclusion
  • Economic sustainability: This is the ability to ensure that the business is financially stable over the long-term as the costs of operation increase
  • Ethical governance: Favorable utilization of AI systems and diligent income tax disclosure

These dimensions have a direct influence on strategic decisions and the priorities of the organization.

16

The Decision Dilemma

With ProPlay approaching the next stage of its expansion, the management has to decide how to strike the right balance between the AI-driven efficiency of its functioning, financial results, payment of the income taxes, effectiveness of the procurement process, and ethical adherence to sporting culture.

Some of the questions facing leadership include:

  • How much should AI-infused insights be used in making strategic and operational decisions?
  • How can the cost of procurement and warehouse be controlled without the service quality being affected?
  • What should be the balance between money and tax and ethical obligations towards the sports development?

The future of ProPlay in the dynamic sports business environment in India will be determined by the decisions made.
